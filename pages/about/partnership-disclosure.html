Partnership disclosure and editorial policy

We believe trust begins with transparency. This page explains our relationship with Klinik St. Georg, how our content is created, and the editorial standards we hold ourselves to.

Our relationship with Klinik St. Georg

Lyme Treatment Germany is an educational platform created in partnership with Klinik St. Georg (St. George Hospital), a private integrative medicine hospital located in Bad Aibling, Bavaria, Germany. Klinik St. Georg has been treating Lyme disease patients since 1994.

This is not a hidden affiliation. We disclose it openly because we believe patients deserve to know who is behind the information they read when making medical decisions.

What this partnership means

  • Lyme Treatment Germany is operated in coordination with the medical team at Klinik St. Georg
  • Our clinical content is informed by Klinik St. Georg's three decades of experience treating Lyme disease
  • When we recommend Klinik St. Georg as a treatment option, we do so based on published evaluation criteria
  • We have a commercial interest in connecting qualified patients with Klinik St. Georg for consultation and treatment
  • This commercial interest does not override our commitment to accurate, balanced medical information

What this partnership does not mean

  • We are not obligated to present Klinik St. Georg's treatments as superior to alternatives
  • We do not suppress information about the limitations or risks of treatments offered at Klinik St. Georg
  • We do not exaggerate outcomes or make claims that are not supported by clinical evidence
  • We do not disparage other treatment approaches or competing facilities

How our content is created

Authorship

Content on Lyme Treatment Germany is developed by a team of medical writers working in consultation with the physicians at Klinik St. Georg. Clinical claims, treatment descriptions, and medical information are reviewed by licensed physicians before publication.

Editorial standards

All content on this site adheres to the following principles:

  • Accuracy — Medical information is based on published research, established clinical evidence, and institutional clinical data. We cite the basis for our claims.
  • Balance — Comparison content presents both (or all) sides fairly. We acknowledge the strengths of alternative approaches and the limitations of ours.
  • Transparency — We clearly label editorial assessments, distinguish them from factual statements, and disclose our partnership on every comparison page.
  • No cure claims — We never claim that any treatment will cure Lyme disease. We present outcomes honestly, including the fact that not all patients respond to treatment.
  • No fear-mongering — We do not exploit patient anxiety or desperation. We present information that helps patients make calm, informed decisions.
  • Patient privacy — Patient stories and testimonials are shared with explicit permission and presented as individual experiences, never as generalizable outcomes.

Editorial labels

Throughout our content, you will see the following labels that distinguish different types of information:

  • Disclosure badge — Appears on comparison pages and any content where our partnership with Klinik St. Georg is directly relevant. Identifies the commercial relationship.
  • "Editorial" label — Marks sections that contain our editorial perspective or assessment, as opposed to purely factual information.
  • "Editorial assessment" label — Marks evaluation tables, ratings, or comparative assessments that reflect our interpretation of available evidence.
  • Evidence level indicators — On clinical content, we indicate the strength and type of evidence supporting each claim (RCT, institutional data, observational, etc.).

External links and references

When we link to external sources, we do so for informational purposes. External links do not imply endorsement by or affiliation with the linked organizations unless explicitly stated. Links to Klinik St. Georg's own website (klinik-st-georg.de) are clearly identifiable as connecting to our partner institution.

Financial disclosure

Lyme Treatment Germany derives revenue through patient consultations and treatment referrals to Klinik St. Georg. This financial relationship is disclosed openly. Our editorial content is not pay-per-referral; we do not receive a commission for individual patient referrals. The content investment is part of a broader commitment to patient education and transparent information.
