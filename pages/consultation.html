What to expect after you submit

We designed our process to be thorough, transparent, and respectful of your time. Here is exactly what happens after you submit your case review.

1
Case review

A physician reviews your submitted information — your symptoms, testing history, and treatments tried — to assess whether our treatment approach may be appropriate for your case.

2
Personal response

Within 2 business days, you receive a personal email from our team with an initial assessment and recommended next steps. This is not an automated reply.

3
Video consultation

If a consultation is appropriate, we schedule a video call with a Lyme specialist physician to discuss your case in detail, answer your questions, and outline treatment options.

4
Treatment plan

If you choose to proceed, we develop a personalized treatment plan with a clear timeline, cost estimate, and practical guidance for traveling to Germany.

Questions about the consultation process

No. The initial case review is provided at no cost and with no commitment. Our medical team reviews your information to determine whether our treatment approach may be appropriate for your situation. If a video consultation is recommended as a next step, we will discuss any associated costs transparently before scheduling.

You will receive a personal response from our medical team within 2 business days. During peak periods, it may take slightly longer, but we will always acknowledge your submission promptly. Our responses are written by our clinical team — they are not automated replies.

If our medical team determines that our treatment approach is not the best option for your case, we will tell you honestly. Where possible, we will suggest alternative directions or resources that may be more appropriate. We believe that helping you find the right path — even if it does not lead to us — is the right thing to do.

Yes. If you are a caregiver, family member, or advocate submitting on behalf of someone with Lyme disease, please include the patient's information in the form. You can note in the treatment history section that you are submitting on their behalf. Our team will follow up with whichever contact information you provide.

Having the following information available will help us provide a more thorough assessment: your primary symptoms and how long you have experienced them, any Lyme disease test results, a list of treatments you have already tried, and any other diagnoses you have received. However, you do not need all of this to submit — share what you can, and we can gather additional details during a follow-up consultation.

This is not an emergency service.
If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately. In Germany, dial 112. In the United States, dial 911. In the United Kingdom, dial 999. This case review is for non-urgent inquiries only.
