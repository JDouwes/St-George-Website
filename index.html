Lyme treatment questions

A gentle medical procedure that raises your core temperature to 41.6–41.8°C under sedation. At this temperature, Borrelia bacteria become structurally unstable. You are monitored continuously in a hospital setting. We have performed this procedure over 30,000 times.
We are honest about this: many patients experience significant improvement. Some achieve full symptom resolution. Some improve partially. And some do not respond as we hope. What we can promise is that we will be transparent about what our experience shows — and what we do not yet fully understand.
A typical treatment program is 2–3 weeks. This includes comprehensive diagnostics, hyperthermia sessions, antimicrobial support, and integrative therapies. The exact plan is tailored to you after your consultation.
Every program is different. We provide a detailed, transparent cost estimate after your initial consultation — before you make any commitment. We also help with insurance documentation for reimbursement.
